ABOUT ME

Who I am, what makes me tick, and what I can do.

I'm Findley Griffiths, aspiring developer with a secondary focus in cybersecurity. If there's a problem I want to be the guy who solves it. My life's journey from engineering, to process improvement, to my Eleven Fifty Academy bootcamp experiences in cybersecurity and software development has led me here. To me, there's not much better than seeing that code work for the first time - to see whatever project I've worked on in action. It's tremendously satisfying to see the finished products of my work.

What I bring to the table is a continuous improvement mindset, a recoginition of the fact that the code we write is ultimately about satisfying the customer's needs and if it doesn't do what the client needs it doesn't matter how pretty it is. Protection of the integrity of data is important, and my goal is to be the person who not only can create apps that work well, but do it in a secure fashion.
